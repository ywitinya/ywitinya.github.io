About Me


Hey there, I'm Yabesi Witinya, a sophomore at Colgate University majoring in Computer Science. I'm on a journey to explore the vast world of computer science, and while I haven't settled on a specific field just yet, I'm excited about the endless possibilities it offers.


Outside the realm of coding and algorithms, I have a passion for soccer. I'm not only a player but also an avid fan of the English Premier League. There's nothing like watching a thrilling match on the weekend to unwind and ignite my competitive spirit.


Family is important to me, and I come from a big one. As the first-born among five siblings, I've always had a level of responsibility and confidence that naturally led me into leadership positions. Currently, I serve as the Vice President of the African Student Union (ASU) at Colgate University, where I work to foster a sense of community and celebration of diverse cultures.



One thing that sets me apart is my exceptional communication skills. I'm proud to be a Colgate Admission Ambassador, where I get to share my experiences with prospective students, guide them on tours, and host webinars. I love connecting with people and helping them navigate the exciting journey of higher education.


In the tech world, I'm versatile. I can code in languages like Java, Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My proficiency extends to other tools like Excel, where I can crunch numbers and analyze data. Technology isn't just my major; it's a passion that I'm eager to apply and share with others.

If you'd like to learn more about me or explore my projects, feel free to visit my LinkedIn or check out my work on GitHub.
